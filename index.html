Who is Burna Boy and why am I a Fan?

Burna Boy is a Nigerian artist who was rised in Ahoada and later moved to Lagos state where he completed his education. he was born in July 2 1991, in Port Hacourt, Rivers state. He is known as "Burna Boy or Giant of Africa" but his birth name is Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu. why am I a fan? First of all I would like to start by saying that I am not a huge fan of Burna Boy because he and I are both Nigerian and share the same birthday, although that's a plus. I love his music because it has gotten me through a lot in my life. His music have strong and hidden messages behind them. When I listened to his recent song "Monsters you made", after listening to this album I instantly became a fan due to the way he decided to send his message across the world with his angelic voice filled with anger, desire for change in Africa, and full of hope. Burna Boy have always been representing Nigeria through his interviews and music but this album showed a different side of him that nobody has never seen. This album touched the hearts of Nigerians and other African countries including the Democrartic Republic of Congo after the #EndSARS movement and the #FreeCongo movement. "Monsters you made" was a sign of hope for most Africans. I love Burna Boy for his thoughtful music and for what he stands for.
